## About This Seminar

As winter melts into spring, the body naturally begins to release accumulated Kapha — the earth-and-water force that has kept us warm and stable through the cold months. Without conscious support, this seasonal shift can leave us feeling sluggish, congested, and heavy.

This one-day seminar gives you the practical tools to work *with* spring's natural cleansing energy: understanding which foods to favour, which herbs support the process, and how to adjust your daily routine so the transition feels energising rather than draining.

## What You Will Learn

- How to identify signs of Kapha accumulation and imbalance
- Seasonal foods and spices that gently reduce Kapha without depleting you
- Herbal support — Triphala, ginger, turmeric, and more
- A simple spring daily routine you can start the next morning
- Practical cooking: preparing a Kapha-reducing lunch together
