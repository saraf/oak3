## About This Seminar

Abhyanga — the Ayurvedic practice of self-oil massage — is one of the most powerful and accessible tools in the entire Ayurvedic tradition. The classical texts describe it as conferring the same benefits as regular exercise: improved circulation, toned muscles, calm nerves, and deep nourishment for every tissue layer.

In this hands-on workshop, you will learn the correct technique from scratch, understand which oils suit your constitution, and leave with the confidence to make Abhyanga a sustainable part of your daily routine.

Places are strictly limited to 8 to allow individual attention.

## What You Will Learn

- The Ayurvedic theory behind Abhyanga — why oil, why daily, why warm
- How to choose the right oil for your dosha and the season
- Step-by-step self-massage technique for the full body, including scalp and feet
- How long to leave the oil on and how to bathe afterwards
- Variations for when you are short on time (a 5-minute version that still works)
- Oil pulling (Kavala) — the oral Abhyanga practice
