## About This Tour

Kerala, in the southwest tip of India, is the heartland of living Ayurvedic practice. The climate, the plants, the culture, the lineages of vaidyas (Ayurvedic physicians) — everything here has shaped and sustained Ayurveda in its most complete form for thousands of years.

This ten-night study tour is designed for those who want to experience Ayurveda not as a spa treatment or a set of techniques, but as a complete living tradition. You will receive authentic treatments, consult with experienced vaidyas, visit herb gardens and pharmacies, attend morning yoga and meditation, and have time to absorb the extraordinary natural beauty of Kerala.

Groups are limited to **8 participants** to ensure an intimate, unhurried experience.

## What Is Included

- 10 nights at a genuine Ayurvedic resort (not a hotel with an Ayurveda add-on)
- Daily Panchakarma or rejuvenation treatments prescribed by the resident vaidya
- Initial and follow-up consultations with the vaidya
- All meals (Ayurvedic diet throughout)
- Ground transport in Kerala for all group activities
- Guided visits to an Ayurvedic herb garden and pharmacy
- One cooking class: Kerala Ayurvedic cuisine
- Morning yoga and pranayama sessions
- Tour leadership by Keiko Yamamoto throughout

## What Is Not Included

- International flights (Osaka ↔ India)
- Travel insurance (strongly recommended)
- Personal shopping and individual excursions

## A Typical Day

Mornings begin early — around 6am — with yoga and pranayama as the Kerala sun rises over the garden. Breakfast is followed by your individually prescribed treatment session, which might last 60–90 minutes. Afternoons are a mix of guided activities (herb garden visits, lectures, cooking class) and free time for rest, swimming, or exploring. Evenings are quiet: dinner together, early sleep. The pace is deliberately slow. This is not a sightseeing tour.

## Who Is This For?

This tour is open to anyone with a genuine interest in Ayurveda — you do not need prior study or experience. It is equally suitable for those who have been studying for years and want to deepen their understanding. Previous participants have included complete beginners, practitioners, yoga teachers, and healthcare professionals.
