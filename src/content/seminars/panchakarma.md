## About This Seminar

Panchakarma — literally "five actions" — is the most celebrated and comprehensive cleansing and rejuvenation programme in Ayurveda. It is the process by which accumulated toxins (Ama) and excess doshas are methodically drawn from the tissues, moved to the digestive tract, and eliminated. Done properly, it can reset the body in ways that daily practice alone cannot reach.

This seminar demystifies Panchakarma: what it actually involves, what the five procedures are, who it is suitable for, what to expect during and after, and how to find a genuine programme versus a watered-down spa version.

## What You Will Learn

- The Ayurvedic theory of Ama (toxin accumulation) and why periodic cleansing matters
- The three stages of Panchakarma: Purvakarma, Pradhanakarma, Paschatkarma
- The five main procedures: Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, Raktamokshana
- Preparatory practices you can do at home (Snehana and Svedana)
- How to evaluate and choose a Panchakarma programme in India or Japan
- What to eat and how to live in the weeks following treatment
