## About This Seminar

In Ayurveda, skin health is an expression of inner balance — particularly the quality of Rasa (plasma), the first and most fundamental tissue. No cream or serum can substitute for what happens at a deeper level, but the right external practices *do* make a genuine difference, especially when they work in harmony with your constitution and the season.

This workshop covers both the theory and the practice: understanding your skin through the lens of the doshas, and learning a set of simple, beautiful rituals you can do at home with accessible, natural ingredients.

## What You Will Learn

- How Vata, Pitta, and Kapha skin differ — and what each needs
- The Ayurvedic approach to cleansing, toning, and moisturising
- Preparing and applying Ubtan — the traditional Ayurvedic facial scrub
- Facial Abhyanga with appropriate oils
- Herbal steaming for different skin types
- Internal care: which foods and habits most affect skin health
