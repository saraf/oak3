## About This Seminar

Ayurvedic herbal medicine is vast — thousands of plants, hundreds of classical formulas, centuries of refined practice. But a handful of herbs stand out as genuinely accessible, well-researched, and transformatively useful for everyday health.

This online seminar introduces six of the most important: how each one works, which constitutions and conditions it suits, how to take it, and — equally important — when *not* to use it. You will come away with a clear, practical understanding you can apply immediately.

## Herbs Covered

- **Ashwagandha** — the great adaptogen; building strength, reducing anxiety, supporting sleep
- **Triphala** — the three-fruit formula; gentle daily detox, digestive health, eye care
- **Brahmi** — the brain tonic; memory, focus, nervous system calm
- **Shatavari** — the female tonic; hormonal balance, hydration, reproductive health
- **Turmeric (Haridra)** — anti-inflammatory, liver support, skin
- **Ginger (Shunthi)** — digestive fire, circulation, nausea, the "universal medicine"
