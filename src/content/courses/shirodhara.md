## About Shirodhara

Shirodhara — the continuous pouring of warm oil over the forehead — is one of Ayurveda's most celebrated and instantly recognisable treatments. The name comes from *shiro* (head) and *dhara* (flow). A thin, steady stream of warm medicated oil is poured over the third eye point in a gentle, rhythmic motion for 30–60 minutes.

The effect on the nervous system is profound: most people enter a deeply meditative state within minutes, and many fall asleep. It is used classically for anxiety, insomnia, headaches, hair and scalp conditions, and to support deeper Panchakarma treatments.

## What You Will Learn

This intensive day course teaches you to perform Shirodhara safely and effectively — both on others and as a self-practice using a simple stand. By the end of the day you will have given and received the treatment under guidance, and will understand the theory deeply enough to practise confidently at home or in a professional setting.

Anyone interested in Ayurvedic treatments — from those wanting to practise on family members at home, to massage therapists and yoga teachers looking to expand their offering. No prior Ayurveda knowledge is required, though familiarity with the doshas is helpful.
