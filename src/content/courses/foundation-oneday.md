## About This Workshop

This one-day workshop is the fastest and most enjoyable way to get a real, practical grounding in Ayurveda. In a single Saturday you will cover the essential theory, discover your constitution, understand your digestion, and learn the self-care practices you can start using the very next morning.

The day is a mix of lecture, discussion, and hands-on practice. Lunch is included — cooked according to Ayurvedic principles and tailored to the season.

## What You Will Take Home

- A clear understanding of your dosha and what it means for your health
- A personalised daily routine outline
- A printed herb and food guide
- A small Ayurvedic product gift
- A participation certificate

## Continuing Your Studies

Many participants go on to join the regular Ayurveda Talk Course after this workshop. If you do, the one-day workshop fee can be credited toward your first term.

## Who Is This For?

Anyone curious about Ayurveda — no prior knowledge is needed. This is the ideal starting point before committing to the longer term course.
