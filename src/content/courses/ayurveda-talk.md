## About This Course

India has a tradition of ancient wisdom passed down through thousands of years — Ayurveda, the science of life, is one of its most complete and practical expressions. The word **AYURVEDA** means "the knowledge that gives birth to life." Even the oldest of India's ancient traditions — from 5,000 to 10,000 years ago — held that imbalance in the five elements causes disease and disturbance in the body and mind, and that this imbalance is entirely preventable.

Adjusting one's life according to the elements of earth, water, fire, air, and space — and tailoring daily life to the seasons — supports physical strength, natural beauty, and resistance to illness. And not just the body: Ayurveda addresses the mind and spirit as well, making it a complete science of human flourishing.

This Talk Course covers **Ayurvedic theory, the three doshas, physical constitution, nutrition, and practical self-care** — the essential knowledge of Ayurveda, taught in an accessible, practical way. Ancient Indian traditional medicine, refined over 5,000 years, is full of wisdom that is as relevant today as ever. This course is your opportunity to make that wisdom your own.

## Course Format

Each session runs for approximately two hours and consists of a 90-minute lecture followed by a 40-minute practical session where you put the theory into practice — whether through cooking, self-care techniques, herb tasting, or group discussion.

The course runs twice yearly (spring and autumn terms). You may join at the beginning of any term; prior knowledge is not required.

## Certificate

Upon completing the full course, participants receive a course completion certificate and a printed herbal reference guide issued by the Osaka Ayurveda Institute.

## Who Is This For?

This course is open to everyone — complete beginners are very welcome. It is equally valuable for those with some Ayurveda background who want a more structured understanding. Previous participants have included homemakers, healthcare professionals, yoga teachers, and anyone simply curious about living more healthily.
